About Durand

Welcome to the Durand Cup, a timeless celebration of football's rich legacy and spirit. Established in 1888, the Durand Cup stands as Asia's oldest football tournament, a revered platform where tradition and passion intersect.

Whether you're a lifelong enthusiast or discovering the magic of football for the first time, the Durand Cup offers an unparalleled experience of excitement and heritage. Join us in this grand celebration, where every goal resonates with history, every cheer amplifies the spirit of the game, and every moment contributes to a legacy of excellence.
